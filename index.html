Topics of Expertise

Available for presenting, workshops, and consulting in the following areas:

🤖

Ethical, High-Impact AI for English Language Teaching

Training educators to design AI-supported lessons and workflows that improve learning outcomes while protecting academic integrity, privacy, and equity. Practical classroom uses of generative AI including custom chatbots for speaking, writing, feedback, and lesson differentiation.

🌱

Positive Psychology & Wellbeing-Centered Language Pedagogy

Supporting teachers with evidence-based positive psychology practices to strengthen student engagement, confidence, and persistence. Routines and task designs that normalize mistakes, increase psychological safety, and build productive learner mindsets.

🎙

Task Design for Speaking, Pronunciation & Fluency

Designing speaking tasks that develop comprehensibility, fluency, and prosodic control through meaningful, level-appropriate communication. Efficient feedback techniques and assessment approaches accessible without specialized phonetics training.

🌍

Intercultural Communication for English-Medium Classrooms

Helping educators build intercultural communication competence through explicit instruction in interactional norms, perspective-taking, and communication repair strategies. Classroom practices that create shared culture in multilingual, multicultural environments.
