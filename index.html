Research

Positive Psychology in the Classroom

Exploring how intentional pedagogical design — gratitude, resilience, and psychological safety — transforms the student learning experience.

🤖

AI & the Learning Experience

Investigating the pedagogical boundaries of generative AI: how it can be used ethically and intentionally to elevate — rather than replace — meaningful learning.

🗣

Heritage Language & Speaking Tasks

Examining how speaking task type and learner attitude shape the language production of heritage Spanish speakers.
